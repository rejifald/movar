Collapse the content-hidden curtain to a single eye symbol at its smallest size. The cover pill's responsive collapse gains a floor tier: on a target too small for even the icon plus one action (short and ≤132px wide), it folds down to just the slashed-eye mark, so a tiny concealed element still shows a clear "hidden" marker instead of an overflowing or clipped pill.
